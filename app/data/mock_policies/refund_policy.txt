Refund Policy

Customers may request a refund within 7 calendar days of delivery if the product is defective, damaged in transit, or materially different from the item description.

To approve a refund, support should verify:
- order or invoice proof
- delivery date
- product condition evidence, if damage is claimed

Refunds may be rejected when:
- the request is made after 7 days
- the item shows signs of misuse
- the uploaded proof does not match the order

If an item arrives damaged, the customer should upload a photo of the damage and packaging where possible.
